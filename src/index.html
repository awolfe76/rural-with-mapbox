About this tool

This tool is maintained by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) to help mortgage lenders determine which properties are located in a “rural” or “underserved” area as defined in 12 CFR 1026.35(b)(2)(iv)(A) and (B).

If more than 50% of a lender‘s total covered transactions are secured by first liens on properties that are located in areas that are either “rural” or “underserved”, the lender may be exempt from certain regulatory requirements under the Truth in Lending Act, such as creating escrow account for certain high-priced mortgages and prohibiting balloon payment provisions with regard to “qualified mortgages” and high-cost mortgages.
